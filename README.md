# forgelog
My Workout Tracker
Leveraging Claude, I created an html workout tracker that I can track on my phone.
In summary it tracks the following and builds a dashboard for me to review my consistency and % complete

Weekly structure
* Monday: Upper body strength + 2‑mile run
* Tuesday: Lower body strength + light core
* Wednesday: Upper body strength + 2‑mile run
* Thursday: Lower body strength + core + light conditioning
* Friday: Full‑body strength (short) + 2‑mile run
* Saturday: Optional “Fighting skills” (mobility, stick/sword drills, shadow sparring)
* Sunday: Rest and recovery

Gym sessions (templates)
Pick weights so the last 2 reps are tough but clean.

Upper body days (Mon & Wed)
* Bench press or dumbbell press: 4 sets × 6–8 reps
* Pull‑ups or lat pulldown: 4 × 6–10
* Overhead press: 3 × 8–10
* One‑arm dumbbell row or cable row: 3 × 8–10
* Accessory (pick 1–2): biceps curls, triceps dips, lateral raises: 3 × 10–12

Lower body days (Tue & Thu)
* Back squat or leg press: 4 × 6–8
* Romanian deadlift: 3 × 8–10
* Walking lunges: 3 × 10 steps each leg
* Calf raises: 3 × 12–15
* Core: plank 3 × 30–45 sec, hanging leg raises or cable crunch 3 × 10–15

Friday full‑body (short)
* Deadlift (or trap‑bar deadlift): 4 × 5
* Incline dumbbell press: 3 × 8
* Pull‑ups or rows: 3 × 8–10
* Farmer’s carry: 3 × 30–40 seconds

Runs and conditioning
* Keep your 2 miles on Mon/Wed/Fri at an easy–moderate pace so they don’t wreck leg days.
* On Thursday, after lifting, add 10–15 minutes of conditioning (rower, bike, or battle ropes intervals like 30 seconds hard / 60 seconds easy).

Progression and intensity
* Aim to add 2.5–5 lb to big lifts whenever all sets feel solid, or add 1–2 reps per set week to week.
* Deload every 5–6 weeks (reduce weights by ~20–30% for one week).
“Fighting Skills flavor” day (Saturday)
* 10 minutes joint mobility (hips, shoulders, spine).
* 15–20 minutes with a stick/wooden sword: basic swings, footwork, shadow “sparring.”
* 5–10 minutes core finisher (Russian twists, Pallof press, side planks).
